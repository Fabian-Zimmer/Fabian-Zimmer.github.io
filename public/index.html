Hi, I am Fabian

About me and this site:

During my academic career path, culminating in a PhD in Physics at the University of Amsterdam, I learned about my aptitude for quickly absorbing, transforming, and disseminating knowledge. My passions include a few areas of science and philosophy. To ruminate and discuss the big question about reality and existence is also sought after daily.

Currently, I endeavour to use my skills to help people/society/humanity in meaningful ways, and to contribute to our ever-growing understanding about who we are and want to be. To get an impression of me, I listed a few strengths and weaknesses, which I see myself having, on the right.

About this website: It is the digital equivalent of my being. It serves as a documentation of my journey so far, as well as an archive for my personal thoughts and writings in the form of posts.

Title: Relic Neutrinos in Silico.
Summary: By developing a simulation code that simulates trajectory of cosmic background neutrinos, their history throughout the evolution of the Universe and their properties today were analysed. Welcome message and context for my posts

Welcome, dear reader. Usually, thoughts are pretty messy, noisy, and transient. The proces of putting them in a coherent order and noting them down in an intelligable way is pretty hard. These posts are my attempts of this process, for topics that I deeply care about, are invested and interested in, and that I think wouldn’t hurt to shape our thought processes a little. Some of them are of a “mainstream” nature, i.e. they are talked about more often in everyday life, whereas some less so. The ones that are less known are the ones that shaped my thinking and decision-making processes the most, and from which I have overall benefitted a lot in my life so far.
